You and Almric reach the cave and can hear the dragon inside.

You take the lead walking into the cave. You have your sword drawn

and are ready for a battle. You enter the cave and come face to face

with Inferno. He roars at you and breathes fire in your direction.

The heat is unbearable. Then Inferno speaks, "Why do you challenge me?"

You respond, "You have been attacking the citizens of Corwin".

"I have been tasked with ending your reign of terror". Inferno responds, "I don't

want to fight. I have riches most humans can only dream of. Leave

me in peace. I will reward you with all the riches you can carry".

 

Decline the dragons offer and prepare for a fight.

Take the gold.
