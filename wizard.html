Almric approaches the fairy and you follow behind.

The fairy notices you and flies behind a tree.

As you get closer to the tree the fairy returns and

she is followed by a man in a pointed hat carrying a staff.

The wizard greets you. He says his name is Vathan and the fairy

is his familiar. The wizard tells you death awaits you if you take

the left path. He says if you want to get to Inferno quickly you

should take the trail to the right.

 

Take the path on the left.

Take the trail on the right.
