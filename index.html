You and Almric find yourselves deep in the forest

on a quest to stop Inferno, a powerful red dragon.

Inferno and his minions have been tormenting the local

populace in the town of Corwin for years. King Andel

commissioned you and Almric, heroes of the land, to find

and defeat Inferno. Before you are two paths leading further

into the forest. The path on the left is dark and foreboding.

The path on the right is bright and welcoming.

 

Take the path on the left.

Take the path on the right.
