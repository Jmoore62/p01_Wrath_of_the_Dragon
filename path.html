You and Almric have come across what seems to be

the most beautiful and quiet path in the forest.

There is wildlife all around. You feel at peace.

Your mount is trying to go quickly, but you reign him in,

wanting to spend more time in this serene place.

After a while, you realize you need to get back to your quest.

You urge your mount into a gallop. Before long you come to

a mountain.

 

Approach the mountain from the east.

Approach the mountain from the west.
