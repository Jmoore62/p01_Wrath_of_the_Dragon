Almric leads the way through a swamp. The smell is

unbearable. You feel like you are going to pass out

if you don't escape the swamp soon. You race ahead of

Almric. After a short time you leave the swampy waters.

Off in the distance to the east you see the silhouette of

a large animal with a horn. To the west you see a horse.

Almric starts towards the horse.

 

Walk towards the creature in shadows.

Follow Almric.
